# Balanced Room Fantasy Levels

**Date**: 2025-01-22  
**Status**: Completed  
**Priority**: Medium  
**Category**: Enhancement  

## Description

Modify the AI room generation prompts to create a more balanced distribution of fantastical vs mundane rooms, making the fantastical areas more impactful by creating contrast with more normal environments.

## Details

### What is the problem?
Currently, all rooms generated by the AI tend to be highly fantastical and magical, which can make the world feel overwhelmingly mystical and reduce the impact of truly special or mysterious areas.

### What should happen instead?
The AI should be guided to create:
- **70% mundane/normal rooms**: Ordinary castle rooms, hallways, storage areas, basic chambers that feel grounded and realistic
- **30% fantastical/magical rooms**: Unique, mysterious, or magical areas that will stand out as special and memorable

This creates a more balanced fantasy world where magical elements feel special rather than commonplace.

### Acceptance Criteria
- [x] Update AI prompts to include fantasy level guidance
- [x] Specify that 70% of rooms should be mundane/normal for the setting
- [x] Specify that 30% of rooms should be fantastical/magical/unique
- [x] Generated rooms show clear distinction between mundane and fantastical areas
- [x] Fantastical rooms feel more impactful due to contrast with normal areas
- [x] Room generation maintains thematic consistency within regions

## Technical Notes

### Implementation Areas
- **Room Generation Prompts**: Update AI prompts in room generation service
- **Probability System**: Consider implementing a system to track fantasy level distribution
- **Thematic Guidance**: Ensure the AI understands what constitutes "mundane" vs "fantastical" for the setting

### Example Guidance for AI
```
Fantasy Level Distribution:
- 70% Normal/Mundane: Standard castle rooms, basic chambers, hallways, storage areas, guard rooms, kitchens, etc.
- 30% Fantastical: Magical chambers, mysterious areas, enchanted rooms, unique architectural features, etc.

For normal rooms: Focus on practical, realistic spaces that serve clear purposes in a medieval fantasy castle.
For fantastical rooms: Include magical elements, mysterious features, or unique architectural details that make them memorable.
```
