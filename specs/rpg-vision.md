# Shadow Kingdom: Complete RPG Vision

**Date**: 2025-01-21  
**Status**: In Development  
**Priority**: High  
**Category**: Architecture  

## 🎯 Core Vision

Transform Shadow Kingdom from a text adventure into a full-featured AI-powered RPG with dynamic content generation, featuring:

- **Combat System**: Turn-based combat with stats, weapons, and armor
- **Inventory Management**: Comprehensive item system with equipment and consumables
- **NPC Interactions**: AI-driven characters with dialogue, trade, and combat
- **Quest System**: Dynamic AI-generated storylines and objectives
- **Character Progression**: Experience, leveling, and skill development
- **World Mechanics**: Locked areas, environmental interactions, and puzzles

## 🌍 Game World Overview

### Core Gameplay Loop
1. **Explore** AI-generated rooms and discover new areas
2. **Interact** with NPCs through natural language dialogue
3. **Combat** hostile creatures using turn-based mechanics
4. **Collect** items, weapons, and armor to improve capabilities
5. **Progress** through quests and unlock new story content
6. **Advance** character abilities through experience and leveling

### Player Experience
- **Text-based interface** with rich, AI-generated descriptions
- **Natural language processing** for intuitive command input
- **Persistent world state** across multiple game sessions
- **Dynamic storytelling** that adapts to player choices
- **Emergent gameplay** through AI-generated content and interactions

## 🎮 Core Game Systems

### 1. Character System
**Player Character Attributes:**
- **Health Points (HP)**: Current and maximum health
- **Attack**: Base damage in combat
- **Defense**: Damage reduction from attacks
- **Level**: Character progression indicator
- **Experience (XP)**: Points toward next level
- **Gold**: Currency for trading
- **Inventory**: Carried items and equipment

**Leveling Mechanics:**
- Gain XP from combat victories and quest completion
- Level up increases stats and unlocks new abilities
- Stat point allocation for character customization
- Class-based progression paths (fighter, mage, rogue, etc.)

### 2. Combat System
**Turn-Based Combat:**
- **Attack**: Deal damage based on weapon + attack stat
- **Defend**: Reduce incoming damage for one turn
- **Use Item**: Consume potions or activate special items
- **Flee**: Attempt to escape from combat

**Damage Calculation:**
```
Damage = (Attacker Attack + Weapon Damage) - (Defender Defense + Armor Defense)
Critical Hit = Random chance for double damage
```

**Combat States:**
- **Initiative**: Determine turn order
- **Status Effects**: Poison, paralysis, buffs, debuffs
- **Death**: Player respawns at last save point, NPCs drop loot

### 3. Inventory & Items
**Item Categories:**
- **Weapons**: Swords, axes, bows (increase attack)
- **Armor**: Helmets, chest, boots (increase defense)
- **Consumables**: Health potions, mana potions, food
- **Keys**: Unlock doors and special areas
- **Quest Items**: Special objects for storylines
- **Treasure**: Valuable items for selling

**Inventory Mechanics:**
- **Weight Limit**: Based on character strength
- **Equipment Slots**: Weapon, armor pieces, accessories
- **Stacking**: Consumables stack, equipment doesn't
- **Durability**: Items degrade with use

### 4. NPC System
**NPC Types:**
- **Friendly**: Merchants, quest givers, townspeople
- **Neutral**: Guards, travelers, scholars
- **Hostile**: Monsters, bandits, creatures

**AI-Driven Behaviors:**
- **Personality**: Each NPC has consistent traits and speech patterns
- **Memory**: NPCs remember past interactions with player
- **Goals**: NPCs have objectives that drive their actions
- **Relationships**: Reputation system affects NPC reactions

**Interaction Types:**
- **Dialogue**: Natural language conversations
- **Trade**: Buy/sell items with merchants
- **Combat**: Fight hostile NPCs
- **Quests**: Receive and complete objectives

### 5. Quest System
**Quest Types:**
- **Main Story**: Primary narrative quests
- **Side Quests**: Optional objectives for rewards
- **Fetch Quests**: Retrieve specific items
- **Kill Quests**: Defeat specific enemies
- **Escort Quests**: Protect NPCs to destinations

**Quest Mechanics:**
- **Dynamic Generation**: AI creates contextual quests
- **Branching Paths**: Player choices affect quest outcomes
- **Rewards**: Experience, gold, items, story progression
- **Journal**: Track active and completed quests

### 6. World Mechanics
**Region-Based World Generation:**
- **Regions**: Thematic areas like mansions, forests, caves generated by AI
- **Distance-Based Probability**: Rooms farther from region center have higher chance of starting new regions
- **Retroactive Center Discovery**: Region centers discovered through exploration, not predetermined
- **Contextual Generation**: AI receives adjacent room descriptions and region context for coherent world building

**Environmental Interactions:**
- **Locked Doors**: Require specific keys or items
- **Hidden Passages**: Discovered through exploration
- **Traps**: Damage or status effects
- **Puzzles**: Logic challenges with rewards
- **Interactive Objects**: Levers, switches, mechanisms

**Special Rooms:**
- **Region Centers**: Most important rooms with unique NPCs, quest items, and story elements
- **Shops**: Merchant locations for trading
- **Temples**: Healing and blessing services
- **Dungeons**: Dangerous areas with valuable rewards
- **Safe Zones**: Areas where combat cannot occur

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

### Database Schema Additions

```sql
-- Regions for thematic world generation
CREATE TABLE regions (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  game_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  name TEXT, -- "Blackwood Mansion", "Whispering Forest"
  type TEXT NOT NULL, -- "mansion", "forest", "cave", "dungeon"
  description TEXT NOT NULL, -- Rich AI context description
  center_room_id INTEGER, -- The discovered center room
  created_at DATETIME DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP,
  FOREIGN KEY (game_id) REFERENCES games(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  FOREIGN KEY (center_room_id) REFERENCES rooms(id)
);

-- Enhanced rooms table with region support
-- ALTER TABLE rooms ADD COLUMN region_id INTEGER;
-- ALTER TABLE rooms ADD COLUMN region_distance INTEGER; -- Distance from region center
-- ALTER TABLE rooms ADD FOREIGN KEY (region_id) REFERENCES regions(id);

-- Characters (players and NPCs)
CREATE TABLE characters (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  game_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  name TEXT NOT NULL,
  type TEXT NOT NULL, -- 'player' or 'npc'
  level INTEGER DEFAULT 1,
  experience INTEGER DEFAULT 0,
  hp INTEGER NOT NULL,
  max_hp INTEGER NOT NULL,
  attack INTEGER DEFAULT 10,
  defense INTEGER DEFAULT 5,
  gold INTEGER DEFAULT 0,
  created_at DATETIME DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP,
  FOREIGN KEY (game_id) REFERENCES games(id) ON DELETE CASCADE
);

-- Item definitions
CREATE TABLE items (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  name TEXT NOT NULL UNIQUE,
  type TEXT NOT NULL, -- weapon, armor, consumable, key, quest, treasure
  subtype TEXT, -- sword, potion, door_key, etc.
  value INTEGER DEFAULT 0, -- sell price
  weight INTEGER DEFAULT 1,
  properties JSON, -- damage, defense, effects, etc.
  description TEXT,
  created_at DATETIME DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP
);

-- Inventory system
CREATE TABLE inventory (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  character_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  item_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  quantity INTEGER DEFAULT 1,
  equipped BOOLEAN DEFAULT FALSE,
  durability INTEGER, -- null for non-degradable items
  FOREIGN KEY (character_id) REFERENCES characters(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  FOREIGN KEY (item_id) REFERENCES items(id)
);

-- NPCs with AI personalities
CREATE TABLE npcs (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  character_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  room_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  disposition TEXT DEFAULT 'neutral', -- friendly, neutral, hostile
  dialogue_state JSON, -- conversation history and state
  ai_personality TEXT, -- AI personality prompt
  movement_pattern TEXT, -- stationary, wandering, patrol
  spawn_rate REAL DEFAULT 1.0, -- respawn probability
  last_interaction DATETIME,
  FOREIGN KEY (character_id) REFERENCES characters(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  FOREIGN KEY (room_id) REFERENCES rooms(id)
);

-- Quest system
CREATE TABLE quests (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  game_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  name TEXT NOT NULL,
  description TEXT,
  objectives JSON, -- list of objectives with completion status
  status TEXT DEFAULT 'active', -- active, completed, failed, abandoned
  rewards JSON, -- xp, gold, items
  giver_npc_id INTEGER, -- which NPC gave the quest
  created_at DATETIME DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP,
  completed_at DATETIME,
  FOREIGN KEY (game_id) REFERENCES games(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  FOREIGN KEY (giver_npc_id) REFERENCES npcs(id)
);

-- Combat encounters
CREATE TABLE combat_encounters (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  game_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  room_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  participants JSON, -- character IDs in combat
  turn_order JSON, -- initiative order
  current_turn INTEGER DEFAULT 0,
  status TEXT DEFAULT 'active', -- active, completed
  created_at DATETIME DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP,
  FOREIGN KEY (game_id) REFERENCES games(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
  FOREIGN KEY (room_id) REFERENCES rooms(id)
);

-- Loot drops
CREATE TABLE loot_tables (
  id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
  source_type TEXT NOT NULL, -- npc, chest, quest_reward
  source_id INTEGER, -- specific NPC or quest ID
  item_id INTEGER NOT NULL,
  drop_rate REAL DEFAULT 0.1, -- 0.0 to 1.0
  min_quantity INTEGER DEFAULT 1,
  max_quantity INTEGER DEFAULT 1,
  FOREIGN KEY (item_id) REFERENCES items(id)
);
```

### Service Architecture

**New Service Classes:**
- `CharacterService`: Manage character stats, leveling, experience
- `CombatService`: Handle turn-based combat mechanics
- `InventoryService`: Item management and equipment
- `NPCService`: AI-driven NPC behaviors and interactions
- `QuestService`: Quest tracking and completion
- `LootService`: Random item generation and drops
- `TradeService`: Merchant interactions and transactions

**Enhanced Existing Services:**
- `RoomGenerationService`: Region-based generation with distance probability and contextual AI prompts
- `RegionService`: Manage region creation, center discovery, and contextual information
- `BackgroundGenerationService`: Generate quests and NPCs with region-appropriate content
- `CommandRouter`: Add RPG commands (attack, equip, trade, etc.)

### AI Integration Enhancements

**Enhanced Grok Prompts:**
- **Region Generation**: Create thematic regions with names, types, and rich contextual descriptions
- **Room Generation**: Contextual room creation using adjacent room descriptions and region themes
- **Center Room Focus**: Special prompts for region center rooms with appropriate grandeur and importance
- **Character Generation**: Create NPCs with personalities and goals appropriate to their regions
- **Quest Generation**: Dynamic storylines based on player progress and regional themes
- **Dialogue Generation**: Contextual conversations with memory and regional flavor
- **Item Generation**: Unique items with properties matching regional themes
- **Combat Descriptions**: Vivid combat narration with regional atmosphere

**Content Consistency:**
- **Regional Coherence**: Adjacent rooms and regional themes maintain logical consistency
- **Distance-Based Importance**: Important NPCs and items more likely in region centers
- **World State Tracking**: AI considers previous generations and regional context
- **Character Memory**: NPCs remember past interactions
- **Story Continuity**: Quests connect to overall narrative and regional stories
- **Balanced Generation**: Items and challenges scale appropriately with regional themes
